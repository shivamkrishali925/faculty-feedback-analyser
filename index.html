📝 Student Feedback Form

Online Feedback Form

Students can submit feedback using the Google Form:

Open Feedback Form →
⚠️ Note: To analyze responses from the Google Form, download the responses as a CSV file from Google Forms and upload it in the "Upload Data" tab.

How to Export Google Form Responses:

  1. Open your Google Form
  2. Click on the "Responses" tab
  3. Click the three dots (⋮) menu
  4. Select "Download responses (.csv)"
  5. Upload the downloaded CSV file in the "Upload Data" tab
